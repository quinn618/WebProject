Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload my notes? expand_more
Go to your profile page, click on "Upload Resource", select your file (PDF, DOCX, or image), add a title and description, and submit. Your document will be reviewed before being published.
How does the payment system work? expand_more
You can recharge your virtual wallet using credit card or PayPal. When you download a paid document, the amount is deducted from your balance. Sellers receive 80% of each sale.
What are "Aura Points"? expand_more
Aura Points are our reputation system. You earn them by uploading quality resources, getting upvotes, and helping other students. Higher Aura = better visibility for your uploads.
Is my uploaded content safe? expand_more
Yes! All uploads are encrypted and stored securely. You retain full ownership of your content. We only display it on our platform with your permission.
How do I report inappropriate content? expand_more
Click the "Report" button on any resource page, or contact our support team directly. We review all reports within 48 hours.
